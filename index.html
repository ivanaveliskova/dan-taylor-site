Competitive Dancesport

Ever dream of dancing like they do on "Dancing with the Stars?" You have come to the right place! Did you know that the San Francisco Bay Area has one of the largest competitive partner-dancing communities in the country? Why not immerse yourself in an exciting and fun challenge! We provide instruction to all sorts of competitors, including Pro/Ams of all ages, amateur and collegiate couples, and formation teams. Our students have performed at local showcases, charity galas, and large competitive events!

How do we stand out? What we offer:

  • Pro/Am rates more affordable than anyone else's.
  • Fleshed out and highly-detailed lesson curriculum.
  • Strong background in movement science and music theory.
  • Clear communication skills and effective instruction ability.

As a couple that became United States Champions within their first year together as well as having produced numerous National and World Champion students, we are confident that we can bestow all the tools needed for you to become the glamorous performer you desire to be.
